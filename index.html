Endospher Body Contouring is an innovative non-invasive treatment that uses a specialized device with rotating spheres to deliver compressive micro-vibrations deep into the skin and underlying tissue. These vibrations stimulate circulation, break down fat deposits, reduce cellulite, and firm and tone the skin. The treatment is painless, requires no downtime, and is suitable for virtually any area of the body — including the abdomen, thighs, arms, and buttocks. Most clients begin to see visible improvements after just a few sessions.
LPG Endermologie uses patented mechanical roller technology to stimulate fat cells, boost circulation, reduce cellulite, and firm the skin — making it ideal for overall body reshaping and smoothing. Venus Legacy, on the other hand, uses Multi-Polar Radio Frequency and Pulsed Electro Magnetic Fields to generate heat in the deeper layers of the skin, promoting collagen production and fat cell reduction. Venus Legacy excels at skin tightening and is especially effective for treating loose or sagging skin. Our specialists can help you determine which treatment — or combination of treatments — is best suited for your goals.
